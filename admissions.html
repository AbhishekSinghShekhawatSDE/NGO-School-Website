Admissions at Gyan Jyoti Special School

Joining our caring and supportive learning community.

Who Can Apply?

Gyan Jyoti Seva Samiti welcomes children who have been diagnosed with various intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our programs are thoughtfully tailored to meet diverse individual needs, ensuring each child receives the specialized care and education they deserve.

We specifically cater to children with conditions including, but not limited to:

  • Intellectual Disability (ID)
  • Cerebral Palsy (CP)
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Slow Learner Profiles
  • Speech Disorders
  • Difficulty in Eating (requiring specific support)
  • Multiple Disabilities

We encourage parents to contact us to discuss their child's specific requirements. Our team is here to help you understand how Gyan Jyoti Special School can be the right place for your child's unique journey.

Documents Required for Admission

To ensure a smooth admission process, please have the following documents ready:

  • Child's Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Child's Medical/Disability Certificate from a qualified professional (Original & Copy)
  • Previous School Records/Reports (if applicable)
  • Parent/Guardian's Aadhar Card or other Photo ID Proof (Copy)
  • Passport Size Photographs: Child (4 copies), Parents/Guardian (2 copies each)
  • Child's Immunization Record (Copy)

Fees & Government Aid Information

At Gyan Jyoti Seva Samiti, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive specialized education, therapies, and care to all our students completely free of charge. Our services are offered at no cost to families, ensuring that every child with special needs has access to quality support regardless of their financial background.

As an NGO, we are committed to making these vital services accessible. While our direct services are free, we are also able to guide eligible families in understanding and applying for various government schemes and financial aid that are available for children with disabilities in India. Our aim is to help you navigate these resources to further support your child's overall well-being.
