About Gyan Jyoti Seva Samiti

Our Story & Purpose

Gyan Jyoti Seva Samiti was founded with a heartfelt mission: to create a nurturing and empowering educational environment for children with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Operating as the Gyan Jyoti Special School in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we firmly believe that every child possesses unique strengths and the inherent potential to lead a fulfilling life. Our core purpose, guided by 'Gyan Jyoti' (Light of Knowledge) and 'Seva' (Service), is to unlock this potential through personalized learning, compassionate care, and a steadfast commitment to celebrating every achievement, no matter how small.

We strive to be more than just a school; we aim to be a truly supportive community for our students and their families. Our dedication fosters holistic growth, promotes independence, and brings immense joy to the learning journey of every child.

Our Guiding Force: The Executive Committee

Driven by a profound commitment to 'Seva' (service) and the belief in the illuminating power of 'Gyan Jyoti' (knowledge), our dedicated Executive Committee established this Samiti. Their collective vision addresses the critical need for specialized, empathetic schooling for children with special needs. They lead us in creating a sanctuary of learning where differences are celebrated, and every child is nurtured to their fullest potential.

Our Holistic Approach & Methodology

At Gyan Jyoti Special School, our educational methodology is profoundly child-centric and rooted in a holistic understanding of each student's needs. We develop comprehensive Individualized Education Plans (IEPs) for every child, crafted in close collaboration with parents, our special educators, and expert therapists. Our curriculum and interventions are designed to cater specifically to children with: Intellectual Disability (ID), Cerebral Palsy (CP), Autism, Slow Learner profiles, Speech Disorders, Difficulty in Eating, and Multiple Disabilities.

Our integrated approach encompasses a wide range of services:

  • Specialized Education: Tailored academic and functional literacy programs.
  • Speech Therapy: Enhancing communication skills and addressing speech disorders.
  • Physiotherapy: Improving gross motor skills, balance, and physical rehabilitation.
  • Occupational Therapy: Developing fine motor skills, sensory integration, and daily living activities for greater independence.
  • Home-Based Programs: Extending support and education to deprived children in their home environment.
  • Early Intervention Programs: Critical support for younger children to mitigate developmental delays.
  • Vocational Training: Equipping older students with practical skills for future self-reliance and financial independence.
  • Behavior Modification: Addressing challenging behaviors through targeted therapies and improving self-help skills.
  • Life Skills & Socialization Training: Fostering positive interactions, self-care, and community integration.
  • Yoga Practice, Cultural & Art/Craft Training: Promoting overall well-being, creativity, and cultural awareness.
  • Health & Wellness: Regular health check-ups and organizing medical camps.
  • Educational Excursions: Providing enriching experiences beyond the classroom.

We maintain a low student-teacher and student-therapist ratio to ensure personalized attention and optimal support for every child, truly embodying the spirit of 'Seva'.

Our Core Values

Our work at Gyan Jyoti Seva Samiti is built upon a foundation of unwavering values:

Compassion & Seva (Service)

Understanding, kindness, and dedicated service guide all our interactions and commitment to our students and their families.

Gyan Jyoti (Light of Knowledge)

We believe in the illuminating power of knowledge and tailored education to unlock every child's unique potential.

Inclusion & Dignity

We are committed to creating a welcoming space where every child feels truly valued, respected, and belongs.

Holistic Development

Focusing on comprehensive growth by nurturing academic, social, emotional, physical, and life skills for overall well-being and independence.

Empowerment

Equipping children with the tools and confidence to navigate their world and achieve self-reliance.
